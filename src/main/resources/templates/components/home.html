Destiny Worship Crew

Crew has been updated to version 2.7. Read all about it in the release notes.

The store is where you can place orderRequest for equipment and supplies for your campus. Each item you request will be forwarded to the appropriate person to provide you with the things you need efficiently.

Second, is Resources.

Here you can read up on the latest information to help you in your ministry. You can post articles for your team and get general help without needing to track down the best resource on your own. There will also be training materials along with Destiny approved operational guides.

Last, we have Support.

We issues arise at your campus, the best way to get help is to submit a ticket. This ensures that everyone who needs to know, does know. You can also keep up-to-date on the progress of your tickets and provide additional information as symptoms progress. Tickets also help give pastors and team leaders an indication of the technical health of each campus.
